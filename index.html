About Me

Zac Morton is a Software Engineer that specializes in the MERN stack and is born and raised in Southern California. He graduated with a BA in Communications from California State University, Fullerton. His relationship with computers began at an early age. As the son of a physics and special education teachers who would bring home a Macintosh Classic computer for the summer. Playing video games such as Odell Lake, The Oregon Trail, and Where in the World is Carmen San Diego developed a passion for learning and pixels.

In 2014, Zac founded, produced, and hosted the LoudGrowl.com Podcast. It was a series of shows built around sports, movies, and video games. LoudGrowl.com is reforming and is slated to restart. Look for it late-2019 early-2020. Currently, he works as Lead Content Strategist with GlamourGraceFaith.com. Taking a fashion brand that had less than a thousand followers three years ago, and developing a steady growth trajectory to where it exists at over fifteen thousand followers. He continues to work with major brands such as Lulus, Vince Camuto, etc. Contact for consultation on content development strategies.

Zac is passionate about education and volunteers his time working with young students and schools to foster an excitement towards technology and photography. Believing this was critical to his development. Most recently, he volunteered for a fundraiser at St. Lucy's school in Long Beach, CA to take pictures of Santa and the student body. In his free time he is a giant sports fan of the Lakers and Dodgers and is sad that Game of Thrones is drawing to a close. He also loves snowboarding, visiting national parks, and keeping up with the latest trends in the world of technology and science.
